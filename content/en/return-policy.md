# Return & Exchange Policy

We strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with your purchases. If you're not satisfied with a product, you can return it according to the following terms.

## Return Conditions

- Return requests must be submitted within **30 days** of receipt
- The product must be in its **original condition** and unused
- The **original packaging** must be included with all accessories
- A **purchase receipt** or order proof must be provided

## Non-Returnable Products

The following products cannot be returned:

1. Custom or made-to-order products
2. Perishable products
3. Digital products after download
4. Opened personal care products

## Return Process

1. **Submit Request**: Contact customer service to submit a return request
2. **Approval**: You'll receive confirmation with shipping instructions
3. **Shipping**: Send the product to the specified warehouse address
4. **Inspection**: We'll inspect the product within 3 business days
5. **Refund**: The amount will be refunded within 5-7 business days

## Exchanges

You can exchange a product for another of equal value or pay the difference. The same return conditions apply to exchanges.
